§THE ARGUMENT

Clinicians are not struggling because they do not know enough. They are struggling because there is not enough time to do what they already know properly.

You already know what good care looks like. You trained for years to deliver it. The problem is not your knowledge or your commitment. The problem is everything else that fills your day before you get to use either of those things properly.

AI does not fix the NHS. It does not solve workforce pressures or give you back the hours that were never there to begin with.

But it can speed up the parts of your job that should not be taking as long as they do. And for a lot of clinicians that time adds up to something meaningful.

01 / EVIDENCE
