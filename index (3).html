Who writes it

"Let's be real — nobody in your Trust is going to train you on this."

Band 7 NHS Occupational Therapist, Barts Health
Trained in professional football academies before healthcare
East London. Grew up here.
Building in health technology

I work in the NHS. I have a caseload. I sit in MDTs. I know what it is like to be told to use a new digital system with zero training and no time.

I have been that clinician staying late to finish notes, chasing referrals that should take five minutes, and sitting in handovers where half the information is missing. AI does not fix the NHS. But it can give you time back.

This newsletter is not written from a co-working space. It comes from the wards, the ward rounds, the referrals and from someone who has always been willing to say the thing out loud that everyone else is thinking quietly.
